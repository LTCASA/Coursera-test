Photosynthesis
Box 1

Photosynthesis is the process of plants using the energy of sunlight to convert water and carbon dioxide into high-energy carbohydrates and oxygen, a waste product

Box 2

Cellular Respiration is the process that releases energy by breaking down glucose and other food molecules in the presence of oxygen.

Box 3

Different types of cellular respiration can occur in plant and animal eukaryote cells.
